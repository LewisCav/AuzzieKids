Auzzie Kidz

The charity that assists the families of children
under the age of 18 years old that are diagnosed with a disability

We have raised over $750,000
and have provided over 150 grants
for very special kids in need

Yearly fund raising activities
including Mercedes Benz Polo
Championships and the Brisbane
Businessman's lunch held each November

Aussie Kidz Charity has received
the Australian Taxation Office
endorsement in May 2001 to allow
donations over $2 to be tax deductible

With Aussie Kidz Charity,
100% of the donations received
are used for the children

Aussie Kidz Charity assists the families of children
under the age of 18 years old that are diagnosed with a disability.

After years of supporting various well known kids charities, Aussie Kidz Charity was started
in early 2000 by a group of individuals disheartened by watching an increasing percentage
by watching an increasing percentage of donations being used to cover overheads instead of
instead of going to the ones who need it most, the Kids.
We are focusing on gaining support and donations through the generosity of Directors, individuals,
corporations, estate trusts and through various yearly fund raising activities.
